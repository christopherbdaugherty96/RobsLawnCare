## Operating Rule
Do not buy equipment only because it looks professional.

Prioritize:
- reliability
- launch readiness
- route efficiency
- realistic recurring demand

## Expansion Rule
Large purchases should happen only after:
- recurring customers exist
- pricing is validated
- transport is reliable
- workflow is stable
- maintenance costs are understood
