# Truck And Transport Plan

## Goal
Create a reliable transport setup for lawn care and power washing equipment without overspending early.

## Current Status
Transport setup is not yet finalized.

## Minimum Viable Transport
The early goal is reliability, not appearance.

Possible launch setups:
- pickup truck
- SUV with trailer
- borrowed trailer setup
- temporary shared transport arrangement

## Transport Requirements
Vehicle setup must eventually support:
- mower transport
- trimmer/blower transport
- fuel transport
- pressure washer transport
- hoses and accessories
- safety equipment
- customer route efficiency

## Trailer Considerations
Trailer may help with:
- equipment organization
- faster loading/unloading
- future route expansion

Trailer is not automatically required before launch.

## Important Rule
Do not buy a large truck or expensive trailer before:
- recurring customers exist
- routes are validated
- startup costs are understood
- revenue targets are realistic

## Fuel Planning
Track:
- estimated weekly fuel usage
- drive-time efficiency
- route density
- maintenance costs

## Route Efficiency Rule
Dense nearby routes are more important than long-distance scattered jobs.
