# Break-Even Model

## Purpose
Estimate the minimum operating level needed for ShortCut to cover startup and operating costs.

## Current Phase
Pre-launch planning.

Figures in this document are placeholders for planning and should be updated with real numbers over time.

## Potential Startup Costs
### Grass Cutting
- mower
- trimmer
- blower
- fuel containers
- PPE/safety gear
- maintenance supplies

### Power Washing
- pressure washer
- hoses/nozzles
- surface cleaner
- water hose setup
- PPE/safety gear

### Transport
- truck or transport setup
- trailer if needed
- fuel costs
- maintenance reserve

### Business Setup
- website/domain
- business registration if applicable
- insurance if applicable
- marketing materials
- business cards/flyers

## Core Operating Costs
Track:
- fuel
- maintenance
- replacement parts
- marketing spend
- software/tools
- transport costs
- equipment repair

## Example Planning Questions
- How many weekly mowing customers are needed to cover fuel?
- How many recurring customers are needed to justify equipment upgrades?
- How much startup cash is required before launch?
- How much reserve should exist before expansion?

## Operating Principle
Recurring route density should become the stable financial foundation before aggressive expansion or large purchases.

## Expansion Rule
Do not scale based only on revenue.

Track:
- profit stability
- route efficiency
- maintenance burden
- equipment reliability
- workload sustainability
