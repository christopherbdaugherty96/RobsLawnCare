# Weekly Revenue Targets

## Goal
Understand how many recurring lawns are needed for meaningful weekly income.

## Example Targets
### 10 recurring lawns
Approximate weekly range:
$400–700

### 20 recurring lawns
Approximate weekly range:
$800–1400

### 30 recurring lawns
Approximate weekly range:
$1200–2100

## Important Variables
Actual income depends on:
- lawn size
- route density
- fuel costs
- equipment costs
- cleanup work
- recurring customer retention

## Key Rule
Dense recurring routes are more valuable than scattered one-time jobs.
