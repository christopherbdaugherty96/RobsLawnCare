# ShortCut Lawn Care

ShortCut Lawn Care is the active brand name for a planned local exterior property service business formerly organized under the RobsLawnCare working identity.

## Current Phase
Pre-launch planning and business system buildout.

ShortCut is not yet an active field-service operation. The current goal is to build the complete operating structure before launch.

## Business Identity
- Active name: ShortCut Lawn Care
- Previous working name: RobsLawnCare
- Service area: Ypsilanti, Belleville, and nearby areas
- Core model: local residential exterior property services
- Initial focus: grass cutting / lawn maintenance
- Secondary planned service line: power washing after equipment, safety, and readiness are confirmed

## Initial Grass Cutting Services
- Weekly mowing
- Biweekly mowing
- Trimming
- Edging
- Spring cleanup
- Fall cleanup
- Overgrowth cleanup

## Planned Power Washing Services
Power washing is a planned service line, not an active offering until the equipment, safety process, pricing, and readiness checklist are complete.

Potential future services:
- driveway cleaning
- walkway cleaning
- patio cleaning
- exterior surface cleaning where safe and appropriate

## Core Goal
Build a dependable local service business with recurring customers, clear communication, dense service routes, and a launch-ready operating system before physical field operations begin.

## Nova Role
Nova may eventually support organization, checklists, reminders, reporting, quote preparation, route planning support, and business visibility.

Nova must not autonomously contact customers, accept jobs, schedule work, make purchases, change pricing, or claim legal/insurance status.
