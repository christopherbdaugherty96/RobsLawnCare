# ShortCut Pre-Launch Roadmap

## Purpose
This document consolidates the current ShortCut direction so the repo stays coherent as the business is built out.

## Current Truth
ShortCut Lawn Care is a planned local exterior property service business.

Current phase:
- pre-launch planning
- operating system buildout
- equipment and transport planning
- website, workflow, and customer process preparation

ShortCut is not yet an active field-service operation.

## Business Direction
ShortCut should launch narrow and expand carefully.

Primary launch focus:
- grass cutting
- trimming
- edging
- recurring residential lawn maintenance
- seasonal cleanup where equipment and readiness allow

Secondary planned service line:
- power washing / pressure washing

Power washing should not be treated as fully active until equipment, safety, pricing, transport, and workflow readiness are complete.

## Target Service Area
- Ypsilanti
- Belleville
- nearby surrounding neighborhoods

## Core Strategy
The business should prioritize:
- recurring residential customers
- dense local routes
- fast and clear communication
- clean work
- before/after proof
- customer reviews
- realistic equipment spending

Avoid early:
- scattered long-distance jobs
- large commercial work
- overbuying equipment
- claiming services or readiness that are not yet true
- software/automation before workflow is proven

## Current Blockers
- truck or reliable transport not finalized
- grass cutting equipment not fully acquired
- power washing equipment not fully acquired
- insurance/legal readiness not confirmed
- customer workflow not fully implemented
- website/quote form not implemented
- payment/invoice process not finalized
- review/photo process not operational

## Nova Role
Nova is intended to become the backend management support layer across Christopher's business ecosystem.

For ShortCut, Nova may support:
- checklist tracking
- lead and quote organization
- reminder generation
- route planning support
- equipment tracking
- reporting and summaries
- business dashboard visibility
- operational document lookup

Nova must not:
- autonomously contact customers
- autonomously accept jobs
- autonomously schedule work
- autonomously make purchases
- autonomously change pricing
- claim licensing or insurance status
- make legal or financial decisions

Christopher remains the final authority for business decisions and external actions.

## Pre-Launch Work Order
1. Keep repo truth honest and current.
2. Finalize launch checklist.
3. Build equipment and truck acquisition plan.
4. Define pricing for grass cutting first.
5. Add power washing safety and service-limit docs before offering it.
6. Build quote form and customer tracking workflow.
7. Prepare website and Google Business Profile truthfully.
8. Prepare before/after photo and review process.
9. Acquire equipment and transport only when the cost path is clear.
10. Soft-launch with controlled local residential jobs.

## Launch Readiness Rule
ShortCut should not operate publicly as a full grass cutting and power washing company until the checklist, equipment, transport, insurance/legal readiness, customer workflow, and safety processes are complete.

## Expansion Rule
Grass cutting and recurring route density come first. Power washing should expand only after readiness is proven.
