# Pricing Model

## Pricing Philosophy
Do not compete only on being the cheapest.

Charge for:
- reliability
- consistency
- responsiveness
- communication
- clean work

## Starter Pricing Direction
### Small Yard
$35–45

### Medium Yard
$45–65

### Large Yard
$65–100+

## Cleanup Jobs
Quoted individually.

## Important Rule
Recurring customers are more valuable than random one-time work.
