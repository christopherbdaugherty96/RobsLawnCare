# Service Ladder

## Stage 1 — Launch Services
- Weekly mowing
- Biweekly mowing
- Trimming
- Edging
- Blowing off sidewalks and driveways
- Spring cleanup
- Fall cleanup
- Overgrowth cleanup

## Stage 2 — Route Expansion
Add after recurring customers exist.

- Mulch refresh
- Bed cleanup
- Shrub trimming
- Leaf cleanup
- Small landscaping cleanup

## Stage 3 — Seasonal Expansion
Evaluate after operations are stable.

- Aeration
- Dethatching
- Snow removal
- Fertilizer coordination or basic fertilizing only if properly prepared and compliant

## Stage 4 — Higher Ticket Work
Only add after equipment, insurance, and confidence are in place.

- Larger property cleanups
- Brush removal
- Commercial maintenance
- Property refresh packages

## Rule
Launch narrow. Expand only after the recurring route is stable.
