# Local Pricing Model

## Purpose
Create a practical pricing framework for ShortCut before public launch.

## Current Phase
Pre-launch planning.

All prices are planning ranges until real local quotes, route density, and operating costs are validated.

## Grass Cutting Pricing Factors
Price should account for:
- lawn size
- trimming needed
- edging needed
- terrain difficulty
- obstacles
- travel distance
- weekly vs biweekly service
- first-cut condition
- overgrowth level

## Starter Pricing Direction
Use these only as planning ranges:
- Small lawn: $35-$45
- Medium lawn: $45-$65
- Large lawn: $65-$100+
- Overgrowth or first cut: quote separately

## Minimum Job Rule
ShortCut should eventually define a minimum service price so small scattered jobs do not become unprofitable.

## Power Washing Pricing Factors
Power washing should be quoted based on:
- surface type
- square footage
- surface condition
- water access
- chemical needs
- risk level
- travel distance
- equipment readiness

Power washing should remain gated until safety, equipment, and service-limit docs are followed.

## Pricing Rule
Do not compete only on being cheap.

Price for:
- reliability
- communication
- clean work
- route efficiency
- realistic equipment and fuel costs

## Review Rule
Update pricing after real jobs reveal actual time, fuel, and difficulty.
