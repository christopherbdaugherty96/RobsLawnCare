# Quote Intake Workflow

## Goal
Convert local leads into recurring lawn care customers.

## Intake Information
Collect:
- name
- phone number
- address
- requested services
- weekly or biweekly preference
- lawn photos
- preferred contact method

## Workflow
1. Customer submits request
2. Review lawn size and condition
3. Respond quickly with estimate or walkthrough request
4. Confirm service schedule
5. Complete first service
6. Store before/after photos
7. Follow up for recurring service

## Important Rule
Fast communication matters.
Most local lawn care customers choose the company that responds reliably and clearly.
