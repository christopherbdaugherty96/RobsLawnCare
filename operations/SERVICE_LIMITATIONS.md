# Service Limitations

## Purpose
Define the boundaries of work ShortCut should and should not perform during early operation.

## Grass Cutting Limits
Do not accept:
- dangerous overgrowth beyond current equipment ability
- unsafe slopes
- properties with hidden hazards
- jobs requiring commercial-scale equipment not available
- work beyond transport capability

## Power Washing Limits
Do not accept:
- roof washing without proper readiness
- high ladder-risk work
- surfaces with unclear damage risk
- chemical-intensive work without proper understanding
- commercial-scale jobs beyond current capability

## Operational Rule
ShortCut should prioritize:
- safe residential work
- repeatable workflows
- nearby route density
- jobs within current equipment and skill level

## Customer Communication Rule
If a job exceeds current readiness:
- explain limitations clearly
- avoid overpromising
- do not pretend capability exists when it does not

## Expansion Rule
Expand services only after:
- workflow stability
- pricing validation
- safety readiness
- equipment readiness
- operational confidence
