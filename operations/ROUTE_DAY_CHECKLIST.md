# Route Day Checklist

## Before Leaving
- Review route order
- Check weather
- Confirm customer addresses
- Load mower, trimmer, blower, fuel, batteries, and safety gear
- Check equipment condition
- Bring water and basic supplies

## At Each Property
- Confirm correct address
- Check gates, pets, obstacles, and lawn condition
- Complete mowing, trimming, edging, and cleanup as scheduled
- Blow off hard surfaces
- Take after photos when useful
- Record notes before leaving

## End Of Day
- Mark all completed jobs
- Log payments or invoices needed
- Record equipment issues
- Refuel or recharge equipment
- Follow up with customers as needed

## Operating Rule
Route efficiency matters. Reduce unnecessary driving and group nearby customers whenever possible.
