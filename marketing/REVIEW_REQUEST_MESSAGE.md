# Review Request Message

## Purpose
Create simple review request language for satisfied ShortCut customers.

## Current Phase
Pre-launch planning.

Use review requests only after real completed work and a positive customer experience.

## Standard Review Request
Thank you for choosing ShortCut Lawn Care. We appreciate the opportunity to work on your property.

If you were happy with the service, a quick review would really help us grow locally.

## Short Text Version
Thanks again for choosing ShortCut Lawn Care. If you were happy with the service, a quick review would help us grow and serve more local customers.

## Follow-Up Timing
Ask for a review:
- after successful service completion
- after customer confirms satisfaction
- after recurring customers have had a good experience

Do not ask for a review when:
- service quality was uncertain
- there was unresolved damage or disagreement
- the job exceeded expectations poorly
- the customer has not responded positively

## Review Goals
Reviews should support:
- local trust
- Google Business Profile visibility
- proof of reliability
- recurring customer growth

## Operating Rule
Earn reviews through reliable work and clear communication. Do not pressure customers or misrepresent completed work.
