# Review Request Message

## Goal
Collect early reviews after successful jobs.

## Standard Message
Thank you for choosing ShortCut Lawn Care. If you are happy with the work, a quick review would help us grow locally in Ypsilanti, Belleville, and nearby areas.

## Short Version
Thanks again for choosing ShortCut Lawn Care. A quick review would really help us grow locally.

## When To Send
Send after:
- successful first service
- recurring customer approval
- cleanup job completion
- positive customer feedback

## Rule
Only ask for reviews after the customer has had a good experience.
