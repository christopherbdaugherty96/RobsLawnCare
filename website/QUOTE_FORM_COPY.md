# Quote Form Copy

## Purpose
Define the minimum information needed for quote requests.

## Form Header
Request a Free Quote

ShortCut Lawn Care serves Ypsilanti, Belleville, and nearby areas.

## Suggested Form Fields
- Name
- Phone Number
- Email (optional)
- Property Address
- Requested Service
- Grass Cutting / Cleanup / Power Washing
- Weekly or One-Time
- Preferred Contact Method
- Additional Notes
- Property Photos Upload (optional)

## Customer Expectation Copy
Submitting a quote request does not guarantee service availability.

ShortCut may contact you for additional information before preparing a quote.

## Response-Time Goal
Respond to quote requests within a reasonable timeframe.

## Truth Rule
Do not promise same-day service, commercial capability, or power washing availability unless operationally true.
